Early life and career

Since he started as ODI captain in 2013, Virat Kohli has led India in 95 ODIs, winning 65 matches while losing 27 matches. Interestingly, Kohli has the best win percentage (70.43) among Indian ODI captains. Under the captaincy of Virat Kohli, India won 15 out of 19 bilateral ODI series and lost four times.
